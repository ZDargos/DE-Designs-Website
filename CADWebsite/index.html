3D Model Creation

Using state-of-the-art Computer Aided Design (CAD) software, we create highly accurate and customized 3D models tailored to your exact specifications. These digital models transform your vision into reality, serving as the foundation for product development and prototyping. We deliver your preferred file formats — including STL, STEP, SLDPRT, SLDASM, OBJ, F3D, and 3MF — and design around critical tolerances to ensure manufacturability and performance.

Engineering Drawings

Our detailed engineering drawings follow industry standards to bring your 3D CAD models to life by providing the precise specifications needed for fabrication. These drawings act as an essential extension of the digital models, translating complex designs into clear, manufacturable blueprints. Whether your project requires machining, 3D printing, or injection molding for tool and mold creation, our technical drawings ensure that every dimension, tolerance, and material detail is accurately conveyed for flawless production.
