SERVICES

3D Model Creation

Using state-of-the-art Computer Aided Design (CAD) software, we create highly accurate and customized 3D models tailored to your exact specifications. These digital models transform your vision into reality, serving as the foundation for product development and prototyping. We deliver your preferred file formats — including STL, STEP, SLDPRT, SLDASM, OBJ, F3D, and 3MF — and design around critical tolerances to ensure manufacturability and performance.

Engineering Drawings

Our detailed engineering drawings follow industry standards to bring your 3D CAD models to life by providing the precise specifications needed for fabrication. These drawings act as an essential extension of the digital models, translating complex designs into clear, manufacturable blueprints. Whether your project requires machining, 3D printing, or injection molding for tool and mold creation, our technical drawings ensure that every dimension, tolerance, and material detail is accurately conveyed for flawless production.

3D Printed Prototypes

Visualize and test your design with high-quality 3D printed rapid prototypes.

About Us

Welcome to Daedalus Engineering Designs! I’m Jack Ceriello, a Mechanical Engineer with a concentration in Aerospace Engineering and a deep passion for innovative design. I am currently studying at Stevens Institute of Technology and have received honors and merit-based awards for my academic achievements.

Through years of experience with CAD across multiple platforms, I’ve honed my ability to create effective engineering solutions that tackle unique problems. Whether it’s designing parts and tools to solve everyday challenges or developing products for companies like yours, I bring a full toolbox of skills and techniques to every project.

At Daedalus Engineering Designs, I focus on ensuring that every part is designed with its intended fabrication process in mind. I have extensive experience working with additive manufacturing (most notably FDM and SLA (resin) 3D printing) and I’ve designed numerous parts optimized for injection molding, laser cutting, and subtractive manufacturing (machining).

My goal is to deliver designs that are not only functional and efficient but also perfectly suited to your production methods. Let’s bring your ideas to life. Whether you need CAD files, engineering drawings, or 3D-printed prototypes, I’m here to help turn your vision into reality.
